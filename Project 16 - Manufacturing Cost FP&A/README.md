# Project 16 - Manufacturing Cost FP&A

Planned Power BI dashboard for manufacturing cost control, standard cost variance, capacity planning, inventory, and product margin.

## Intended Business Questions

- Which products, plants, or lines are driving margin variance?
- How much cost pressure comes from material, labor, overhead, yield, scrap, and utilization?
- Where should operations focus to protect EBITDA?

## Planned Dashboard Pages

- Manufacturing FP&A Overview
- Standard Cost Variance
- Yield, Scrap, and Capacity
- Inventory and Working Capital
- Product Margin Deep Dive

## Skill Signals

- Cost accounting and FP&A analysis
- Variance bridge design
- Operations finance dashboarding
- Manufacturing margin diagnostics
