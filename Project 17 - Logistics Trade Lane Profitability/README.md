# Project 17 - Logistics Trade Lane Profitability

Planned Power BI product for logistics and freight profitability by customer, trade lane, service, office, and shipment profile.

## Intended Business Questions

- Which lanes, customers, and services create or destroy margin?
- How do fuel surcharge, carrier cost, demurrage, claims, and utilization affect profitability?
- Which customers should commercial teams reprice or renegotiate?

## Planned Dashboard Pages

- Logistics Profitability Overview
- Trade Lane Margin
- Customer and Service Profitability
- Cost Driver Bridge
- Pricing and Action Queue

## Skill Signals

- Logistics domain analytics
- Customer and lane profitability modeling
- Cost-to-serve analysis
- FP&A action storytelling
