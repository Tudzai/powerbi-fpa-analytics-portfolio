# Project 13 - Regional FP&A Consolidation

Planned Power BI product for regional FP&A consolidation across multiple countries, entities, currencies, and business units.

## Intended Business Questions

- Which countries and entities are driving revenue, EBITDA, and cash variance?
- How much variance comes from volume, price, mix, FX, and intercompany eliminations?
- Which markets need management attention before the regional close package is finalized?

## Planned Dashboard Pages

- Regional Executive Overview
- Entity and Country P&L
- FX and Intercompany Bridge
- Business Unit Drilldown
- Close Exceptions and Commentary

## Skill Signals

- Regional FP&A reporting
- Multi-currency and FX translation logic
- Consolidation and elimination thinking
- Executive variance storytelling
